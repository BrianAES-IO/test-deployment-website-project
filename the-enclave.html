Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly does "Air-Gapped" mean?
An air-gapped network means the computers and storage drives holding your most critical data have no physical or wireless connection to unsecured public networks (like the open internet). Because there is a physical gap, hackers cannot reach it remotely to deploy ransomware.
Will The Enclave slow down our daily operations?
No. AESURUS engineers the architecture so that your team experiences a frictionless workflow. While the backend security is incredibly complex, the user experience is seamless, requiring only standard MFA approvals for authorized personnel.
How does this protect us legally?
By ensuring Data Sovereignty, your information is stored locally and is subject only to local laws. This prevents foreign governments from subpoenaing your data through international cloud providers (like Amazon or Microsoft) without your knowledge.
Is The Enclave compliant with the JDPA?
Absolutely. The Enclave was built specifically to exceed the stringent requirements of the Jamaica Data Protection Act (JDPA). Our architecture secures consumer data privacy, preventing leaks and massive regulatory fines.
